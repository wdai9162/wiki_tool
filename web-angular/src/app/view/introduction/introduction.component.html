WIKIPEDIA ANALYSIS TOOL

With the development of the Internet, information retrieval and analysis functions have become the secret of rapid development of various industries. This tool is a Wikipedia data analysis, which contains an overall analysis, individual article analysis, and author-based analysis.

In this part, some information will be displayed. Like the article with longest and shortest history, the article with largest or smallest group of registered users, and articles with highest or lowest number of revisions

Meanwhile, two graph will show the details of the consist of user group and the number of revision edited by the group of users in each year
